Types of Marine Insurance Coverage

At SD Life Wealth, we offer a range of marine insurance coverage options to ensure comprehensive protection:

  • Hull and Machinery Insurance: Covers physical damage to the vessel and its machinery.
  • Cargo Insurance: Protects against loss or damage to goods in transit by sea.
  • Protection and Indemnity (P&I) Insurance: Covers third-party liabilities, including injury, loss of life, and damage to other vessels or property.
  • Freight Insurance: Covers loss of freight revenue due to insured perils.

Benefits of Marine Insurance

Marine insurance provides extensive coverage, protecting you against a variety of risks, including:
